The prefix Y is reserved for Australia. For many Australian ICAO codes, the second letter indicates which Flight Information Region the airport belongs to — B Brisbane, S Sydney, M Melbourne and P Perth. However, the Sydney and Perth FIRs no longer exist and have been merged into the Brisbane and Melbourne FIRs, although the airport codes remain unchanged .
