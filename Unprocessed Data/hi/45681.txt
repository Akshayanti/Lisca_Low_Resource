नाडोल राजस्थान के पाली जिले की देसूरी तहसील का एक नगर है। यहाँ स्थित आशापुरा माता के मंदिर में देशभर से श्रद्धालु आते हैं। 
2001 की भारतीय जनगणना के अनुसार नाडोल की जनसंख्या 9,020 है जिसमें 4,437 पुरुष और 4,583 महिलाएं है।
Nadol was originally called Naddula. The Chahamanas of Naddula ruled the town and its surrounding areas during the 10th-12th century CE. Their founder was Lakshmana was a prince of the Shakambhari Chahamana dynasty. He carved out a principality at Nadol, while his brother Simharaja ascended the ancestral throne. Nadol was ruled by his descendants until Jayatasimha was defeated by the Ghurids. Later, the Jalor Chahamana king Udayasimha captured Nadol. The area was captured by the Delhi Sultanate after अलाउद्दीन खिलजी defeated Udayasimha's descendant Kanhadadeva.
